Hello, my name is Andy Truong

I am aspiring to be a software engineer when I am finished with my studies at Portland State University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. In terms of narrowing down my career interests, I would love to work in both the frontend which is in charge of the UI of the application and backend that deals with the behind the scenes server and database management; ultimately being a full-stack engineer.
My genuine curiosity to continue learning is a great asset when it comes to becoming a better software developer. As a software engineer, it is inevitable that technologies, skills, frameworks… etc will continue to modernize. The ability to learn and pick up new techniques is essential for getting acquainted with the company that I will be working at in the future, while staying up-to-date with the newest technologies.
As a software engineer, I would ideally be working in a team setting where I can count on my teammates to be a reliable ear to bounce ideas and troubleshoot technicalities that may come up in the creation or maintainability of functions/applications. In return, I would do the same for my teammates because I live by the motto, “Treat others the way you want to be treated yourself.”
Outside of academics, I have a great interest in statistics and math, largely due to how often problems both in the real and virtual world can be broken down into these areas of mathematical science. With these interests, a hobby I enjoy is a game titled Teamfight Tactics which is an auto-battler which emphasizes the optimization of statistics game to game to have a higher rank on the leaderboard. In this game, I have peaked in the top < 1% since the game's release in June of 2019.
